Moi-Moi

description

moi moi is also known as bean cake is a highly proteinous food that takes no time to prepare

Ingredients

  • beans
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • seasoning
  1. soak beans for 10 minutes
  2. drain water and pound lightly to remove the skin
  3. separate the skin from the beans
  4. blend beans to a smooth paste
  5. add all the ingredients and scoop into aluminium foil
  6. place foil into boiling water and cook for 20 minutes
