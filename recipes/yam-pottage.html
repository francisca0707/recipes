Yam pottage

Description

yam pottage is a very easy delicious dish to make.

Ingredients

  • yam
  • water
  • onions
  • pepper
  • salt
  • palm oil
  • seasoning

Steps

  1. peel and yam and cut into cubes
  2. put yam cubes in pot and add water to the level of the yam in the pot
  3. add all the ingredients and put the pot on the stove
  4. cook till yam is soft and serve
