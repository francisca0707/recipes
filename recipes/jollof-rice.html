Jollof-rice

Description

This is a favourite recipe for parties and 0ccasions in Nigeria. Any party where jollof-rice is not served is regarded as incomplete.

Ingredients

  1. rice
  2. water
  3. meat of choice
  4. seasoning cube
  5. onions
  6. crayfish
  7. tomato
  8. salt
  9. vegetable oil
  10. pepper
  11. curry and thyne

Steps

  1. first of all, put a clean pot half-filled with water on fire
  2. wash and pour rice into pot and boil for like 5 minutes and drain the water
  3. next, blend tomato and pepper together, slice the onions
  4. wash and put meat in pot and season with curry,salt, thyne and any other seasoning of choice, cook till done
  5. put empty pot on fire, add enough quantity of oil, add and fry onion for like 1 minute
  6. add the blended tomato and also fry till the soured taste has gone
  7. add enoogh water to cook the rice, add the meat broth,salt to taste and the crafish
  8. cook rice till all the water has dried. Remove from fire and serve.
